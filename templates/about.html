About Us

Our Mission

At CafeSpotter, we believe finding the perfect cafe shouldn't be left to chance. We're passionate about connecting coffee lovers, remote workers, and social butterflies with cafes that perfectly match their needs - whether that's reliable WiFi for a day of work, plenty of power outlets for your devices, or clean restroom facilities.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike standard review sites, CafeSpotter focuses on the practical amenities that truly matter when choosing a cafe. Every listing in our database includes detailed information about:

  • WiFi Availability: Connection speeds, reliability, and login requirements
  • Power Situations: Number of accessible outlets and their locations
  • Workspace Details: Table sizes, chair comfort, and noise levels
  • Facilities: Clean restroom availability and accessibility features
  • Digital Integration: Interactive maps showing exact locations
  • Visual Previews: High-quality photos of interiors, exteriors, and seating options
  • Menu Highlights: Specialty coffee offerings and food options
  • Operating Hours: Up-to-date schedules including holiday adjustments
  • Crowd Patterns: Peak hours and quieter times for focused work

Our Story

CafeSpotter was born from frustration. Our founder, a remote worker, spent countless hours searching for cafes with reliable internet and power outlets, only to arrive and find neither available. After one too many disappointing experiences, the idea for a comprehensive cafe database was born. Starting in 2023 with just 50 cafe listings in our local city, we've grown to feature over 5,000 verified locations across the country, with user contributions adding more daily.

How We Gather Information

Our data comes from three trusted sources:

  1. Our Review Team: Our dedicated reviewers personally visit cafes to document amenities and test facilities
  2. Cafe Owners: Establishments can claim their listings and provide verified details
  3. Community Contributions: Our users update information and submit new discoveries
  4. Every submission undergoes verification before appearing on our platform, ensuring accuracy and reliability.
